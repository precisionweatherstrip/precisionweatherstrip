TRI FIN PILE WEATHER STRIP [TRIFIN SL UV] - EXTRUDED, SILICONISED & UV STABILISED

Silicone treated & UV stabilized multifilament yarn ultrasonically welded on solid extruded base along with central and external fins.

Ultrasonic welding of the brush and fin makes it highly durable. UV-stabilized, silicon-treated materials ensures the product is robust, maintains its shape, and has a long service life.

It gives highest level of sealing performance by reducing air infiltration and water penetration therefore, enhances weather resistance making it highly energy efficient, sound proof.
